# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language.
- Trolling, insulting comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards
of acceptable behavior and may take corrective action in response to behavior
that violates this code.

## Scope

This code applies within all project spaces and public spaces when an
individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers by opening a private
security report (see `SECURITY.md`) or by emailing
`otilio.latam@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits,
issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
